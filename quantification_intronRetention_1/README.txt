All raw and processed sequencing data generated in this study 
have been submitted to the NCBI Gene Expression Omnibus (GEO; https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/) 
under accession numbers GSE159919 for poly(A)+ RNA, 
GSE159944 for total RNA, GSE159971 for small RNA, 
and GSE159993 for poly(A)+ RNA in Ptbp knockdown experiments.

Below provided one example of raw data
* GEO: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSM4850331
* SRA: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sra?term=SRX9349423
* Run: https://trace.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Traces/sra/?run=SRR12883492

The pipeline for processing the fastq data is available in RNAseq_Analysis_Pipeline.py.
